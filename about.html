ABOUT US

Inspired by the Blue Lock anime, we are a CTF team dedicated to pushing the boundaries of cybersecurity. Just like the protagonists who strive for soccer excellence, we relentlessly pursue perfection in every challenge we face.

Our team consists of professional hackers who specialize in multiple domains of cybersecurity, combining individual expertise to form an unstoppable force in the competitive CTF arena.

We believe in the Blue Lock philosophy - only the best can survive. Through intense competition and continuous learning, we hone our skills to become the ultimate cybersecurity specialists. Each member brings unique expertise, and together, we form an unbeatable team that dominates every competition.
